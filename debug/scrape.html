Polo Field Usage

The cycle track at the Polo Field may only be used when the Polo Field is not in use. Following is a list of dates and times when the Polo Field will NOT be in use and the track is available for cycle activities.

In the event of a special circumstance like a field closure due to saturation from rain, the Polo Field will not be in use and the track will be available for cycle activities. For information on field closures due to rain, visit https://sfrecpark.org/507/Field-Rain-Out-Info

The Cycle Track will be open the following dates and times:

2024

Monday, January 1 through Sunday, February 25

The Cycle Track will remain open - Polo Field Closed


EXCEPT:


  • Monday, January 22 and Tuesday, January 23 - Partial closures of the track in the morning for asphalt repairs.
  • Saturday, February 24 from 7:45 a.m. to 4:45 p.m. and Sunday, February from 7:45 a.m. to 3:45 p.m. when track will be closed for a sports tournament.


Monday, February 26 through Sunday, May 26

Youth and Adult Sports Programs Begin. The Cycle Track Will be Open:

Mondays all day

Tuesdays*, Wednesdays, Thursdays* and Fridays before 2 p.m. and after 6:45 p.m. (*On Tuesdays beginning March 12, the cycling track will be open after 8:45 p.m. On Thursdays beginning March 14, the track will be open after 8:45 p.m.) 

Saturdays before 7 a.m. and after 6:45 p.m.

Sundays before 7 a.m. and after 6:45 p.m.

EXCEPT:


  • Sundays, from March 3 thru May 12, when track will be open before 10 a.m. and after 6:45 p.m.
  • Sunday, May 19 when track will be open all day with the field closed due to the Bay to Breakers event

Monday, May 27 through Friday, June 7

The Cycle Track will remain open - Polo Fields Closed 

 

Saturday, June 8 through Sunday, June 9

The Cycle Track will be open after 7 p.m. 


Monday, June 10 through Sunday, July 28

Summer Camps, Youth and Adult Sports Programs will take place on the field. The schedule is forthcoming. 

EXCEPT:


Saturday, July 6 and Sunday, July 7 from 7:45 a.m. to 4:45 p.m when track will be closed for a sports tournament.



Monday, July 29 through Friday, August 23

The Cycle Track will be closed for Outside Lands Load in, Event and Load Out and Polo Fields Concert Event and Load Out

Saturday, August 24 through Monday, September 2 

The Cycle Track will remain open - Polo Fields Closed

 

Tuesday, September 3 through Wednesday, November 27 

Fall Youth and Adult Sports Programs Begin. The Cycle Track Will be Open:

Mondays all day

Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays before 2 p.m. and after 6:45 p.m.

EXCEPT:

  • Friday, September 13 when track is closed from 7:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. for Sacred Heart Walkathon
  • Friday, October 4 when track is closed all day for Hardly Strictly Bluegrass
  • Wednesday, November 20 when track is closed from noon to 6 p.m. for SFUSD Cross Country Finals

 

Saturdays and Sundays before 7 a.m. and after 6:15 p.m. EXCEPT:

  • Saturday, October 5 and Sunday, October 6 when track is closed all day for Hardly Strictly Bluegrass


Thursday, November 28

The Cycle Track Closed for Turkey Trot from 7 a.m. to 11 a.m.

 

Friday, November 29 through Tuesday, December 31

The Cycle Track Will be Open Mondays through Sundays all day
